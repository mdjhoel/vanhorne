About St Andrews Trail

Fan site

Welcome to the unofficial fan site of the Saint Andrews Van Horne Trail. This trail is a ~10km loop around town, combining paved rail trail with historic streetscapes and access to areas of natural and tourist interest.

Sir William Van Horne

The trail is named after Sir William Van Horne, the president of the Canadian Pacific Railway and famous summer resident of Saint Andrews. His home (Covenhoven) and farm are national historic sites can be visited at low tide on Ministers Island.

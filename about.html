About St Andrews Trail

Fan site

Welcome to the unofficial fan site of the Saint Andrews Van Horne Trail.

Description

This trail is a ~10km loop around town, combining paved rail trail with historic streetscapes and access to areas of natural and tourist interest.

Who are we?
Sir William Van Horne

Legendary builder of the Canadian Pacific Railway and famous summer resident. We hav big hopes for his trail!

Kurt Gumushel

Town councillor, trail evengelist and shaper of the next generation. He is also our local Facebook influencer.

Mark Hoel

Our webmaster and local fresh air fiend. Mark is on the trail most days, running, biking or otherwise chilling.
